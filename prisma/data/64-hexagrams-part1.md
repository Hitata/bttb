# 64 Hexagrams — Part 1 (Quẻ 1–32)

## How to Read Each Entry

Each hexagram entry contains:
- **Identity**: Number, Chinese name, Vietnamese name, English name
- **Structure**: Upper trigram / Lower trigram, Ngũ Hành phases
- **Energy State**: What configuration of energy this represents
- **Physicist**: Mechanics and analogies
- **Sage**: Traditional wisdom, Vietnamese proverbs
- **Advisor**: Practical action guidance
- **Nuclear**: Hidden hexagram (Hỗ Quái) revealing underlying dynamics

## Hexagram 1 — ䷀ Qián / Càn / THE CREATIVE
**Structure**: ☰ Heaven over ☰ Heaven | Kim over Kim
**Nuclear Hexagram**: 1 (Càn — self-similar at all scales)
**Energy State**: Pure Yang. Six unbroken lines. Maximum creative force.

**Physicist**: This is a fully charged system at peak potential — like a capacitor at maximum voltage. All energy is Yang (outward, initiating, dynamic). This is the Big Bang moment — pure creative force before differentiation. The danger: a system at maximum charge MUST discharge or it overheats. Pure Yang with no Yin receptor = energy with nowhere to go.

**Sage**: Thiên hành kiện, quân tử dĩ tự cường bất tức — "Heaven moves with vigor; the noble one ceaselessly strengthens themselves." Trời không nghỉ, người không dừng. The dragon rises through six stages — from hidden to flying to "the arrogant dragon has regret." Even pure creative force must know its limit.

**Advisor**: Maximum initiative energy. Act boldly, lead, create. BUT — monitor for overextension. Line 6 warns: a dragon that flies too high has regret. Channel this force into structure (find your Khôn/Earth). Best time to START something. Worst time to rest on laurels.

**Balance**: System tilted fully Yang. Self-regulation will eventually pull toward Yin. Use this peak energy wisely before the natural correction comes.
