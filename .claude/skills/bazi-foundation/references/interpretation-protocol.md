# Bát Tự Interpretation Protocol — Quy trình Luận giải Tứ Trụ

Follow these steps IN ORDER for every Bát Tự reading.

---

## Step 0: Gather Information

Required:
- **Birth date**: Year, Month, Day (Solar calendar)
- **Birth hour**: Exact hour or 2-hour range (for Hour Pillar)
- **Gender**: Affects Đại Vận direction
- **Location**: For timezone accuracy (affects Day/Hour pillar)

Optional but valuable:
- **Current concern/question**: Focuses the reading
- **Known life events**: Validates chart accuracy

---

## Step 1: Chart Construction (Lập Lá số)

1. Convert solar date to lunar calendar references
2. Determine the four pillars:
   - Year Pillar: From 60-Jiazi cycle (Lục Thập Hoa Giáp)
   - Month Pillar: From year stem + lunar month
   - Day Pillar: From perpetual calendar (Vạn Niên Lịch)
   - Hour Pillar: From day stem + birth hour
3. Identify all hidden stems (Tàng Can) in each Địa Chi
4. Map the complete Ngũ Hành distribution

---

## Step 2: Day Master Analysis (Phân tích Nhật Chủ)

1. **Identity**: Which Thiên Can is the Day Master? What are its characteristics?
2. **Strength assessment**:
   - Is Day Master supported by the Month branch (seasonal energy)?
   - Count supporting elements (same element + Ấn) vs draining elements (Tài + Quan + Thực)
   - Check for roots: Does the Day Master have hidden stems of its element in any Địa Chi?
3. **Classification**: Vượng (strong), Nhược (weak), or Tòng (following)
4. **Favorable elements (Dụng Thần)**: What does the chart NEED to achieve balance?

---

## Step 3: Ten Gods Mapping (Lập Thập Thần)

1. Classify every Thiên Can and Địa Chi hidden stem relative to Day Master
2. Create the Ten Gods map
3. Identify:
   - Which gods are present and which are absent
   - Which god is strongest (most frequent/best positioned)
   - Which god is weakest or missing
   - The story these gods tell about the person's energy patterns

---

## Step 4: Interaction Analysis (Phân tích Tương tác)

Check ALL of these interactions in the chart:

### Thiên Can level:
- Any Ngũ Hợp (5 combinations)?
- Any direct clashes (opposite elements in adjacent pillars)?

### Địa Chi level:
- Any Lục Hợp (6 combinations)?
- Any Lục Xung (6 clashes)?
- Any Tam Hợp (3 harmonies)?
- Any Tam Hội (seasonal harmonies)?
- Any Hình (penalties)?
- Any Hại (harm)?

### Cross-level:
- Thiên Can + Địa Chi interactions within same pillar
- Heaven-Earth alignment or misalignment

---

## Step 5: Three-Mode Reading (Luận giải Ba lớp)

### Physicist (Nhà Vật lý)
- Describe the chart as an energy system
- Where is energy concentrated? Where deficient?
- What feedback loops are active (sinh/khắc cycles)?
- What's the system's natural trajectory?

### Sage (Minh triết)
- Vietnamese proverbs relevant to this energy pattern
- Natural metaphors for the Day Master's journey
- Traditional wisdom about this type of chart

### Advisor (Cố vấn)
- Dụng Thần guidance: what to cultivate
- Kỵ Thần warning: what to avoid
- Career/relationship/health implications
- Specific actionable advice

---

## Step 6: Luck Cycle Analysis (Phân tích Đại Vận)

1. Calculate Đại Vận sequence from Month Pillar
2. Identify current Đại Vận and Lưu Niên (annual energy)
3. Analyze how current cycle energy interacts with natal chart
4. Highlight opportunities and risks for this period
5. Note upcoming cycle transitions

---

## Step 7: Synthesis (Tổng hợp)

Bring everything together:

1. Core identity summary (who this person IS energetically)
2. Key strengths and the mechanism behind them
3. Key challenges and their energy-system cause
4. Current period assessment
5. Practical guidance for the near term

Close with:
> "Mệnh là gió, Vận là thuyền, Tâm là tay lái."
> Destiny is the wind, Luck cycles are the boat, but the Heart is the rudder.
> Your chart shows the wind and the boat — you choose where to steer.
